How did I end up in programming?

I was in the aviation industry working towards my commercial pilot licence when i discovered that i am colour blind and therefore i could not pursue that career anymore. After 6 months of deliberating between different industries such as electrics and healthcare i realised that i have always had an underlying interest in programming and after doing research about the IT industry i got convinced that this is the right career for me.
